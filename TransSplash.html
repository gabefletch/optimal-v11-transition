Optimal is an open-source library of
user-modified iOS and Android apps
designed to remove their paywalls.
‍
Apps and games on Optimal use two
methods to eliminate a one-time
purchase or subscription, either
by removing payment checking
entirely or spoofing that a
payment has been made when
one has not.
‍
We're still working on the latest
and greatest version of Optimal.
